What we do

Scrap items have no value in the life of people. Infact, they end up wasting up space inside the house. Customers are often looking to part ways with these items, and they end up throwing them away or giving them away for a minimal price. Not only do we offer timely pickup of scrap items, but also we provide our customers money in exchange for it.

Schedule

Customers schedules the pickup through their mobile app / web portal, by providing us the address of pickup.

Pickup

Our pickup boys arrive at their doorstep at the scheduled time for picking up the scrap items.

Payment

Our on-field manager initiates payment after segregation of scrap items. We are going cashless by paying only through UPI / NEFT.

Store

The collected scrap items for the day are stored safely in our warehouses, unless they are dispatched to the industrial vendors for sale.

Sell

A dedicated portal where industrial vendors sign up and place their required order quantity and quotation, subject to availability in our inventory.

Manage

A 1-click portal for tracking every minute aspect of the business and activities of our employees at each level of hierarchy
